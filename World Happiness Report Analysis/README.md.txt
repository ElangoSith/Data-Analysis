Title: World Happiness Report Analysis
Author: Sitharthan Elango

Description: In this data analysis project, I have used the dataset on the state of the global happiness gathered in 2017 to analyze whether economy, health and geopraphy of countries correlate with their happiness score.

Goal: I would like to find out whether certain factors of a country correlate with their happiness scores.

Research Question: Is there a strong correlation between economy, health, geographic factors and the happiness scores of countries?

Technolgies:
- Excel and R for data cleaning.
- R for data visualization
